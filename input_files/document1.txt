Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone introduces Harry, a young boy who discovers he is a wizard. He attends Hogwarts
School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, makes friends with Ron and Hermione, and learns about his past. The school year is
filled with magical classes, Quidditch, and encounters with strange creatures. Harry learns about the dark wizard Voldemort, who killed his
parents but mysteriously failed to kill him. The climax involves the Philosopher's Stone, an object granting immortality. Harry, Ron, and
Hermione navigate dangerous obstacles to stop Voldemort from returning. In the end, Harry confronts Professor Quirrell, who is possessed by
