The Harry Potter series follows the journey of Harry, a young wizard who discovers his true heritage and confronts dark
forces. From the Philosopher’s Stone, where he learns about magic, to the Chamber of Secrets, where he battles a basilisk
and uncovers Tom Riddle’s diary, the story grows darker. In the Prisoner of Azkaban, Harry learns about Sirius Black and
his parents’ past. The Goblet of Fire introduces international wizarding competition but ends with Voldemort’s terrifying return. In the Order
of the Phoenix, Harry faces skepticism from the Ministry of Magic and suffers the loss of Sirius. The Half-Blood Prince
deepens the mystery of Voldemort’s Horcruxes and ends with Dumbledore’s death. Finally, in the Deathly Hallows, Harry and his friends
abandon Hogwarts to destroy Horcruxes, leading to the Battle of Hogwarts. Harry sacrifices himself, survives, and ultimately defeats Voldemort. The
series explores themes of friendship, love, sacrifice, and the battle between good and evil, concluding with peace restored in the
